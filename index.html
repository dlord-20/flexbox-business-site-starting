Why MolEvo?

A massive amount of data is produced every day from analytical laboratories worldwide. A large portion of this data is aimed to characterize complex liquid mixtures.

Value Proposition

MolEvo is a software solution that allows for transforming complex and indecipherable feedstock chemical analysis into a discrete ensemble of molecules.

MolEvo Uniqueness

MolEvo uses a proprietary extension of the evolutionary algorithm allowing the user to create customized datasets for a variety of applications.

Applications of MolEvo

Liquid fuels will continue to play a significant role in the energy sector with conventional fuels possibly declining while newer fuels gain momentum. As the production of cleaner energy becomes paramount, several processing steps are required. However, many mixtures have diverse compositions making characterization complex, difficult, and time- and data-consuming.

As a tool designed to measure physical properties, optimize processes, or enhance research quickly and thoroughly, MolEvo can avoid complex analysis usually performed in analytical labs and give access to a discrete representation of the mixture in molecular structure. By fully characterizing a mixture, only then can it be burned and utilized more efficiently and cleanly.

Our Mission

To provide and develop reliable, versatile, and innovative software methods to serve the needs of the energy community.
